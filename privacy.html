Privacy Policy

OverUnder: Football Prediction App

Your Privacy Matters: OverUnder is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We only collect minimal data necessary to provide our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Identifier: A randomly generated anonymous ID stored locally on your device to identify your subscription status and provide personalized service.
  • Push Notification Token: If you enable notifications, we collect your device token through Firebase Cloud Messaging to send you match predictions and live signals.
  • Purchase Information: When you subscribe to our premium plans, Apple processes the payment. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status, not your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To manage your subscription and unlock premium features
  • To send push notifications about new predictions and live match signals
  • To improve our app and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your device identifier is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage (UserDefaults). We use Firebase, a Google service, for real-time data delivery and push notifications. Firebase complies with industry-standard security practices.

We do not store any personal information on external servers. Match predictions and signals are delivered in real-time and are not linked to your identity.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For real-time database and push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases. Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your user ID in the app's Settings screen.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data, including your device identifier.
  • Opt-out: You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
